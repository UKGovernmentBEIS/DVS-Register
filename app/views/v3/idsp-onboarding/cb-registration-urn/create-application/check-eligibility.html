Check before you start Information you will need

To complete this application you will need the information and documents listed below. You can save your application progress at every step and return to complete at a later time.

You will need the digital identity provider's:

  • Registered name
  • Unique reference number
  • Trading name
  • Companies House number
  • Address
  • Telephone
  • Email
  • Website

Documents and information related to the digital identity provider's certification:

  • Role types
  • Schemes
  • Identity profiles
  • Link to certificate of conformity
  • Upload of certificate of conformity
  • Certificate of conformity issue and expiry date
  • Link to schedule of certification
  • Upload of schedule of certification
  • Schedule of certification issue and expiry date
