Check eligibility

You can use this service to:

  • Create a new application for an IDSP
  • Track your ongoing applications

To start a new application, you'll need:

  • the IDSP Trading Name
  • the IDSP Company Name
  • Company Address
  • Company contact details (UK Telephone number, email address)
  • Schemes certified against
  • URL Link to Certification Document

The IDSP Company Documents that you'll need:

  • Companies House
  • Legal Status
  • Directorships
  • Foreign Funding
  • Financial Probity
  • T/A address
  • Operations Address
  • Sanctions List
  • OFSI
  • UNFC (where applicable)
  • EC (where applicable)
  • TARIC (where applicable)
  • Existing accreditations
  • CAB webpage
