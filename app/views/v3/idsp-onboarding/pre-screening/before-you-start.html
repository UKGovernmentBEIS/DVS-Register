Before you start



We will now ask you a few questions about your eligibility to be on the DVS register. You will need to pass the pre-assessment stage in order to get a certification for the purposes on being

To complete the pre-assessment application form, you’ll need to provide information about your company’s:

  • Registered name
  • Registration number
  • Email
  • Website
  • UK telephone number
  • UK address
  • Availability of a published privacy statement
  • Published service description
  • A warranty statement
  • A liability statement
  • Document proof of your financial capability and physical resources to undergo full certification process and maintain for 2 years
  • Document proof of your financial capability to maintain ongoing quality service
  • Status of your compliance and conformity to internationally-recognised standards
  • Standards (or equivalents) like the GPG 44, GPG 45, Transaction Monitoring Guidance, Biometrics Guidance, and ISO 27018


This application should be filled out by the Senior Authorising Officer at your organisation.
