Submit a pre-assessment application as a digital identity provider



Once you have decided to apply to be on the DVS Register, you must submit a pre-assessment application. Too apply, you must:

  • be a registered company with Companies House in the UK or, if your parent company is international, one that is equivalent in your jurisdiction
  • be in the process of having a registered office in the UK

You will be assessed by the Office of Digital Identities and Attributes to determine suitability to proceed with the certification process. You will receive a confirmation from the office once the initial assessment has been completed.

You will receive confirmation from the Office once the review of your pre-assessment application has been completed.
